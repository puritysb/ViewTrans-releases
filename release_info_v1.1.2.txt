Installation Command:
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/puritysb/ViewTrans-releases/main/install.sh | bash

Manual Installation:
1. Download ViewTrans-v1.1.2-macos.tar.gz
2. Extract the archive
3. Move ViewTrans.app to /Applications/
4. Run: xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/ViewTrans.app
